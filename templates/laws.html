⚖ Women Safety Laws
⚖ Section 354 IPC
Protects women against assault or criminal force with intent to outrage modesty.
🚫 Section 376 IPC
Strict punishment for rape including imprisonment and penalties.
🗣 Section 509 IPC
Punishes words, gestures, or acts intended to insult a woman's dignity.
🏠 Domestic Violence Act (2005)
Protects women from physical, emotional, and economic abuse at home.
💼 Workplace Harassment Act (2013)
Ensures safety of women at workplace and prevents sexual harassment.
📞 Women Helpline (1091)
Emergency helpline number for immediate police assistance.
